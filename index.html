About Me

I am a Versatile Embedded Systems & Single-Board Computer Engineer, AI and Machine Learning Developer with a strong passion for advancing research and innovation. I am proficient in Python, SQL, C/C++, Java and various data engineering techniques. Skilled in designing and implementing complex algorithms for embedded systems, with a focus on leveraging AI and Deep Learning methodologies to tackle real world challenges.

My higher educational journey began with the completion of the National Diploma Programme and I obtained a honor and certification in National Diploma in Electrical and Electronic Engineering Technology at Kwara State Polytechnic Ilorin, a renowned institution known for its commitment to excellence in technical education. During this transformative period, I acquired a solid foundation in Electrical and Electronic Engineering principles and developed a passion for innovation and problem-solving.

However, before I was admitted into the polytechnic which was right after I completed my secondary education, I started an apprenticeship in a privately owned organization called Muyisonic Mobile Link, popularly known and accredited for its expertise in Mobile Phone Software and Hardware Troubleshooting and Repair while I spent three (3) years to learn and came out the best and outstanding apprentice and most of the works was done by me.

Maintaining this strong foundation before and after I graduated from the Polytechnic, with the talents and skills I have had, my journey began and continued respectively in the realm of Embedded Systems Engineering, where I honed my skills in designing and implementing robust, efficient systems that seamlessly integrate with the physical world.

Building upon this strong foundation, I furthered my academic pursuits and I proudly hold a Bachelor of Engineering, in Electrical and Electronics Engineering degree from the esteemed Federal University of Technology Minna, renowned for its excellence in engineering education and technology.

Over the years, I've had the privilege of working on a myriad of projects, from bare-metal firmware development to complex IoT solutions, each imbued with its own set of challenges and opportunities for innovation.

Driven by an insatiable thirst for knowledge, I ventured into the captivating realm of Airtficial Intelligence and Machine Learning , captivated by the prospect of imbuing machines with the power to learn, reason, and adapt autonomously.
As an AI and Machine Learning Developer, I've had the privilege of exploring the depths of neural networks, delving into the nuances of algorithms, and leveraging the power of data to unlock insights that drive meaningful impact.

In addition to my passion for AI and Embedded Systems, I possess a strong foundation in Data Engineering, adept at architecting scalable data pipelines, designing efficient databases, and extracting actionable insights from vast troves of information. Proficient in Python and SQL development, I wield these tools with precision and finesse, transforming ideas into reality and orchestrating harmonious symphonies of code.
